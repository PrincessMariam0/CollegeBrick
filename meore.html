Build The Perfect College Application.

CollegeBlock serves as a cohort advising platform for high school students and college majors that best prepares them in the application process for international colleges. The curriculum of the CollegeBlock is structured across three pillars: skill-building, capacity-building, and community service. The process includes essay writing sessions, test preparation seminars, mock interview sessions, volunteering opportunities, leadership development, and much more. While CollegeBlock does not guarantee admissions, it is designed to assist self-organized and independent students around the world to have a better chance of admissions to international colleges or universities.
